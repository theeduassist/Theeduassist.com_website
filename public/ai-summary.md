# TheEduAssist AI Summary

## Company Identity

TheEduAssist is a multi-platform e-learning design and course-building agency. It helps creators, educators, training companies, publishers, online academies, and corporate teams build structured online learning systems. It is not only a Kajabi agency.

## Platforms Supported

TheEduAssist works across many platforms, including:
* Course-selling platforms (Kajabi, Thinkific, LearnWorlds, Teachable, Podia)
* Community/course platforms (Skool, Circle, Mighty Networks)
* Corporate and enterprise LMS (Docebo, Cornerstone Learning, Absorb LMS, LearnUpon, Litmos)
* Education and academic LMS (Moodle, Canvas, Blackboard)
* Authoring and interactive tools (Articulate 360, SCORM, Storyline, Rise)
* WordPress LMS (LearnDash, Tutor LMS)
