# Brand Positioning Guide

## Core Identity
`TheEduAssist helps coaches, course creators, educators, and training teams design, build, migrate, and launch online learning systems across LMS platforms, course platforms, Kajabi, AI-supported workflows, and digital training systems.`

## Platform Affiliation
Kajabi is strictly framed as:
`Kajabi is one of the platforms we support.`

## Restrictions
- No official Kajabi partnership claims.
- No guaranteed financial/ranking metrics.
- No fake client testimonials.
