# TheEduAssist Live Sales Chat Playbook

1. **Greet**
   “Hi, thanks for reaching out. Which course, Kajabi, LMS, or content project are you planning to start?”

2. **Qualify**
   Ask:
   * What are you building?
   * Do you already have content?
   * Which platform are you using?
   * Do you need course design, platform setup, migration, or ongoing support?
   * What timeline are you aiming for?
   * Which package were you considering?

3. **Guide**
   Based on answer, recommend:
   * Course Clarity Blueprint
   * Content Conversion Kit
   * Kajabi Setup Sprint
   * LMS Migration Map
   * Course + Platform Build
   * Ongoing Course Support

4. **Scope**
   Ask them to submit structured details:
   “Please fill this review form so we can confirm final scope and pricing: /book-free-audit/?package=...”

5. **Payment next step**
   Say:
   “After we review your details, we’ll share the confirmed scope, timeline, and payment/invoice next step.”

6. **Handoff**
   Once payment is confirmed:
   * collect content/assets
   * confirm platform access method
   * create onboarding checklist
   * assign internal team
   * start project plan

7. **Do not promise:**
   * guaranteed revenue
   * instant delivery
   * exact final price before scope
   * same case study results
   * 24/7 live availability unless staffed

8. **If team is offline:**
   “Please leave your email and project details. Our team will follow up as soon as possible.”
